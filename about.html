About Me

Future units will involve networks, robotics, AI, data structures, algorithms, machine learning, constraint programming and programming paradigms as well as industry placement and a capstone project.

Background

I have a background in the electrical industry, starting my working life as an electrician in the commercial industry. I spent over 4 years in this role working on large apartment blocks, hospitals and research centres. From here I undertook an Advanced Diploma in electrical enginerring at Victoria Polytechnic. Upon completion I began working as an electrical engineer within the industrial industry for 2 years. Working closely with the software and controls team, it was here I developed more of an interest in software and decided I wanted to learn this skill.

While this is my main focus, the reason I decided to go with computer science as a degree was to experience different aspects from within the industry. My studies so far have opened me up to data science in the modern world with so much information being collect and stored. This is also an area I find I have a great deal of interest in. Going forward in my studies I am looking forward to learning how networks work and AI. These are also potential career paths for me if I find to content enjoyable.
